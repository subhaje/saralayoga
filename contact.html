Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking, classes, and our services. Don't see your question? Feel free to contact me directly.

How do I book my first session?
You can book your first session by calling me directly at +91 9945400870, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. I'll discuss your needs and schedule a convenient time.
What should I bring to my first class?
Just bring comfortable clothing that allows movement, a water bottle, and an open mind! I provide all necessary yoga props and equipment. If you have your own mat, feel free to bring it.
Do you offer trial sessions?
Yes! I offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation where we can discuss your goals and I can assess your current level. This helps me create the perfect practice plan for you.
Can I switch between online and offline sessions?
Absolutely! Many of my students mix online and offline sessions based on their schedule and preferences. Package deals allow flexibility between different types of sessions.
What if I have physical limitations or injuries?
I specialize in therapeutic yoga and work with students with various physical limitations. During our consultation, please inform me about any injuries or conditions so I can tailor the practice safely.
How far in advance should I book sessions?
For regular sessions, I recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance. For weekend slots or popular times, booking a week ahead is advisable. Last-minute bookings may be available depending on my schedule.
Do you offer home visits?
Yes, I offer home visits for personal training sessions within a 10km radius of J.P. Nagar. There may be a small travel charge for locations beyond 5km, which we'll discuss when booking.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept cash, UPI payments (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), bank transfers, and offer flexible EMI options for annual packages. All payments are processed securely.
