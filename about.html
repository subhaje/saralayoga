My Yoga Journey

My journey with yoga began over a decade ago when I was struggling with stress and anxiety from my corporate job. What started as a simple attempt to find some peace and flexibility became a life-transforming experience that changed everything.

Through consistent practice, I discovered not just physical strength and flexibility, but mental clarity, emotional balance, and a profound sense of inner peace. This transformation inspired me to share this gift with others, leading me to pursue professional certification and training.

Today, I'm passionate about helping others discover their own path to wellness through yoga, whether they're taking their first steps or looking to deepen their existing practice.

My Teaching Philosophy

Personalized Approach

Every student is unique, and I believe in tailoring my teaching to meet individual needs, goals, and physical capabilities. No two yoga journeys are the same.

Safe & Inclusive Practice

I create a welcoming environment where students of all levels, ages, and backgrounds can practice safely and feel supported in their growth.

Holistic Wellness

Yoga is more than just physical exercise. I integrate breath work, meditation, and mindfulness to promote complete mind-body wellness.

Empowerment Through Practice

My goal is to empower students to develop their own practice, build confidence, and discover their inner strength through consistent yoga practice.
