Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pricing, packages, and payment options. Don't see your question? Feel free to contact us directly.

Can I mix different types of sessions in a package?
Yes, absolutely! Our packages are flexible and allow you to mix online classes, personal training, and group sessions according to your preferences and schedule.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for session cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours will be charged 50% of the session fee. No-shows will be charged the full session fee.
Do you offer any discounts for students or seniors?
Yes, we offer a 10% discount for students with valid ID and senior citizens (65+ years). This discount applies to individual sessions and packages.
Can I pause my package if I'm traveling?
Yes, you can pause your package for up to 30 days in a calendar year. Please inform us at least 7 days in advance to arrange the pause.
Are there any additional fees or hidden costs?
No, all prices listed are final with no hidden fees. The only additional cost might be travel charges for home visits outside a 5km radius, which will be discussed before booking.
