Yoga Styles I Teach

I offer instruction in various yoga styles to provide you with a well-rounded practice that can be adapted to your needs and preferences.

Hatha Yoga

Traditional yoga practice focusing on physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama). Perfect for building strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.

Vinyasa Yoga

Flowing sequences that connect breath with movement. Dynamic practice that builds heat, improves cardiovascular health, and enhances flexibility.

Yin Yoga

Passive, meditative practice with longer-held poses targeting deep connective tissues. Excellent for stress relief and improving flexibility.

Restorative Yoga

Gentle, supported poses designed for deep relaxation and stress relief. Uses props to support the body in comfortable positions for extended periods.

Meditation & Mindfulness

Guided meditation sessions and mindfulness practices to cultivate mental clarity, emotional balance, and inner peace.

Pranayama

Breathing techniques and exercises that help regulate the breath, calm the nervous system, and enhance overall well-being.
